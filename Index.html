Innohep® Syringes/Vials

Anticoagulant Tinzaparin sodium for subcutaneous injection

Formulations

10 Vials of 2 ml:

  • Tinzaparin sodium 10,000 IU anti-Xa/ml, preserved with benzyl alcohol (10 mg/ml).
  • Tinzaparin sodium 20,000 IU anti-Xa/ml, preserved with benzyl alcohol (10 mg/ml), stabilized with sodium metabisulphite.

10 Pre-filled syringes with needle safety device:

  • Volumes of 0.25ml, 0.35ml, or 0.45ml containing Tinzaparin sodium 10,000 IU anti-Xa/ml.

2 Pre-filled syringes with needle safety device:

  • Volumes of 0.5ml, 0.7ml, or 0.9ml containing Tinzaparin sodium 20,000 IU anti-Xa/ml, stabilized with sodium metabisulphite.
